Crystal Gems Collector!

How to Play: You will be given a target number at the start of the game. This number is chosen at random.

There are four crystal gems below. By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Here's the catch: you won't know this amount until after you click on a gem.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. You lose if your total score goes above the target number. Choose your gems wisely...

The values of each gem will reset and randomly regenerate each time the game restarts, so with each turn you will need to tweak your strategy. Good luck!
